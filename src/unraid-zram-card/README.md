**Unraid ZRAM Card** displays the status of your ZRAM swap devices directly on the Unraid Dashboard.<br><br>

**Features:**<br>
- **Real-time Stats**: See Used vs. Total ZRAM usage.<br>
- **Compression Ratio**: Monitor how effective your ZRAM compression is.<br>
- **Visuals**: Clean, native-looking dashboard card with a donut chart.<br>
